About University

Ranked among the Top Universities in MP

Sardar Patel University is a State Private University established under the Madhya Pradesh Niji Vishwavidyalaya (Sthapana Avam Sanchalan) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2011 (Madhya Pradesh Act No. 26 of 2011). The Act received the assent of the Honorable Governor on 23rd July 2018, as published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette (Extraordinary) on 28th July 2018.

The University is governed by a Board of Governors, chaired by Shri Diwakar Singh, Honorable Chairperson of Vindhya Shiksha Samiti, Sardar Patel Campus, Gaykhuri, Balaghat (M.P.), who also serves as the first Chancellor of Sardar Patel University.

About SPU

SPU has been establish to create world class quality education in various fields of General, Technical, Professional and Medical education in Madhya Pradesh in particular & India and overseas in general. SPU shall endeavor to promote the study of national integrity, social justice, secularism and democratic way of life, international understanding & scientific approach to the problems of the society in totality
